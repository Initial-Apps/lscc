ABOUT ME
Dr. Dhawan has been in practice for 30 years and has published over 30 articles in dermatology journals. He has a strong interest in general dermatology- acne, psoriasis, eczema, skin growths, precancers, skin cancers, preventative therapy; unusual and rare skin rashes and skin conditions; cosmetic dermatology including fillers, lasers, and Botox; skin cancer surgery. Dr. Dhawan also runs the largest clinical research organization in dermatology in Northern California and has been involved in over 80 trials for new and innovative therapy in dermatology. He is a strong believer in a holistic approach to the skin!
