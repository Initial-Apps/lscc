Patient-centered medical care with Board-Certified dermatologists

Leading Skin Care Centers provides patient-centered medical care and surgical outpatient care with Board-Certified dermatologists who are dedicated to patients. We see patients for both common and complex conditions such as:

Acne

Acne is a chronic skin condition which starts with hair follicles clogged with oil and dead skin cells. It typically presents as whiteheads, blackheads, pimples, small cysts and nodules.

Atopic Dermatitis/Eczema

Commonly presented as itchy rash, atopic dermatitis can affect patients of all ages, although more common in younger kids and elderly. Inadequate control of atopic dermatitis can severely affect the quality of life in patients.

Benign Skin Tumors

Mostly commonly seen are skin tags, seborrheic keratoses, dermatosis papulosa nigricans, epidermal cysts and sebaceous hyperplasia. Although benign in nature, these growths can cause irritation and are cosmetically displeasing. They can be treated with minor surgical procedures.

Contact Dermatitis

Contact dermatitis is an itchy rash caused by external exposure to some particular chemicals. The chemical either causes irritation to the skin or triggers allergic reactions in some patients.

Hair Loss/Alopecia

Common types of alopecia are alopecia areata, androgenetic alopecia, telogen effluvium, anagen effluvium, lichen planopilaris, folliculitis decalvans, dissecting cellulitis and central centrifugal cicatricial alopecia (CCCA).
